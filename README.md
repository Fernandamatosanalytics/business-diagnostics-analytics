# Business Diagnostics Meets Applied Analytics

Applied Analytics & Business Intelligence case study using Python, SQL, and Power BI.

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates how business diagnostic data can be transformed into structured analytical intelligence.

Based on an anonymized consulting framework, the analysis evaluates organizational maturity across five business dimensions:

- Strategy
- Finance
- Marketing
- Operations
- People Management

The project combines financial analysis, data structuring, clustering techniques, and business intelligence visualization to identify operational gaps and strategic improvement opportunities.

## Analytical Techniques

- Data Cleaning & Normalization
- Gap Analysis
- Descriptive Statistics
- Organizational Maturity Scoring
- K-Means Clustering
- Elbow Method
- Silhouette Score Validation

---

## Key Insights

The analysis identified distinct organizational maturity profiles and operational bottlenecks across multiple business dimensions.

Main findings included:
- Strategic planning deficiencies
- Lack of financial KPI structures
- Working capital management risks
- Opportunities for predictive analytics and BI implementation
