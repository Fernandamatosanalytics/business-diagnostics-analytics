# Business Diagnostics Analytics

## Project Overview
This project presents a business analytics case study focused on identifying operational inefficiencies, financial patterns, and strategic improvement opportunities through data-driven analysis.

## Objectives
- Analyze business performance indicators
- Identify financial and operational inefficiencies
- Generate actionable business insights
- Support strategic decision-making

## Tools Used
- Excel
- Google Sheets
- Financial Analysis
- Business Analytics
- Data Interpretation

## Key Insights
- Identified operational inefficiencies affecting performance
- Evaluated financial behavior and business trends
- Proposed strategic recommendations based on analytical findings

## Business Impact
This case study demonstrates analytical thinking, financial reasoning, reporting interpretation, and problem-solving skills applied to business decision-making.
